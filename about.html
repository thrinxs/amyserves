Meet the Founder

From a Vision to a Group of Brands

Chiamaka — known professionally as Amy — is a business leader, HR specialist, and entrepreneur based in Abuja, Nigeria. With years of experience in workforce management and business operations, she identified a gap that most Nigerian SMEs face: the need for reliable, professional support across cleaning and people management — without the cost or complexity of large firms.

AmyServes was her answer. What began as a focused HR consulting practice grew into a group of specialist brands — each solving a specific business problem, all united under one umbrella. Today, AmyServes operates two active brands serving clients across Nigeria: Pura-Kle'N for professional cleaning, and Yourfirm Consults and Integrates Ltd for HR and workforce consulting.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2020

AmyServes Founded

Chiamaka (Amy) launched AmyServes in Abuja with a clear mission — to give growing Nigerian businesses access to professional support services under one roof.

2021

Yourfirm Consults Established

Yourfirm Consults and Integrates Ltd was formally established as the HR consulting arm — delivering workforce management, training, and staffing solutions to Nigerian businesses.

2022

Pura-Kle'N Launched

The professional cleaning brand Pura-Kle'N was launched to address the growing demand for reliable, professional cleaning in Abuja offices and homes.

2024

2,000+ Staff Milestone

Yourfirm crossed a major milestone — over 2,000 staff managed across client businesses, with ₦200M+ in payroll processed nationwide.

2026

Expanding Nationwide

AmyServes continues expanding its reach — new clients, new cities, and new service offerings built on the same professional foundation.
