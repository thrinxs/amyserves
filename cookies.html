This Cookie Policy explains how AmyServes ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website (amyserves.vercel.app). It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, keep you signed in, and understand how you use the site.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

TypePurposeExamples
Essential Required for the site to function — e.g., keeping you signed in or remembering items in a form Authentication tokens (Supabase)
Functional Remember your preferences and choices Language, theme, dismissed banners
Analytics Help us understand how visitors use the site, so we can improve it Vercel Analytics, anonymous traffic counters

We do not currently use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by services we use to power our site, including:

  • Supabase — for user authentication and account management;
  • Vercel — for hosting and basic usage analytics.

These third parties operate under their own privacy and cookie policies.

4. How to Manage Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device;
  • Delete some or all of them;
  • Block cookies from specific sites or all sites;
  • Be notified when a new cookie is set.

Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent parts of the site (such as logging in or accessing dashboards) from working properly.
